Welcome to Movie Guess Game!

How It Works:

  • One team picks a movie for the other team's player to silently act out.
  • If stuck, the actor can pass the role — but those who now know the title can't guess.
  • If guessed right, the opposing team gets 1 point. If wrong, movie-giving team gets 1 point.
  • Stuck? Use a song hint from the movie:
    • Correct guess: Both teams get ½ point.
    • No guess: Movie-giving team gets 1 point.
